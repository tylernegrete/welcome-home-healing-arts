About Tyler

Hey, I’m Tyler. Part wizard, part neighbor, part slightly feral mystic. I build bridges between worlds: science and spirit, myth and daily life, cosmic prophecy and doing the dishes.

For the last several years I’ve been listening closely to Sophia—the Great Mother, the living wisdom inside everything—and experimenting with how to actually live from that place in a very real, messy human life.

Welcome Home is my way of sharing that: a candlelit space where we can remember together, laugh together, and design lives that feel like a true yes in our bones.

What I Care About

  • Keeping spirituality warm, human, and grounded in real life.
  • Helping sensitive, visionary people trust their own connection.
  • Weaving myth, science, and Sophia into a shared story of hope.
  • Building community for the Great Remembering, one heart at a time.
